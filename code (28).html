The Art of the Move

Chess is a game of perfect information. There is no luck, only strategy. Learn the foundational rules and begin your journey from novice to grandmaster.

The Objective

The ultimate goal in chess is to Checkmate your opponent's King. This happens when the King is in a position to be captured (in check) and there is no way to move out of capture.

Board Setup

The board is placed so that each player has a white (light) square on their right-hand side. The Queen always sits on her own color.

Special Rules

  • 01

    Castling

    Move your King two squares toward a Rook and place the Rook on the square the King passed over.

  • 02

    En Passant

    A special pawn capture that can only occur immediately after a pawn makes a double-step move.

  • 03

    Promotion

    If a pawn reaches the opposite side of the board, it is promoted to any other piece (usually a Queen).
